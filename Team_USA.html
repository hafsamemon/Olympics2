Team USA - Overview

The USA have a strong track record at the Olympic Games having won a total 2,400 medals at the Summer Olympic Games. This makes Team USA the most successful team at the Olympics. At Rio 2016, the USA will have competetitors in over 30 sports entering.

Fun Facts

  • US sportsman, Michael Phelps is the most decorated Olympian of any nation with 22 Olympic medals, 22 of which are gold
  • Team USA has entered every modern Summer Olympic games except the 1980 Moscow Olympics which it boycotted
  • The USA has hosted 4 Summer Olympic Games
